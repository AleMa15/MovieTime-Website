List of Rom-com Movies

  • Life as We Know It (2010)

    A young couple dies from a car crash, leaving their young baby to their best friends, a woman and a man who should raise the child as their own. The movie is a comedy.

  • The Switch (2010)

    A romantic comedy about a man who accidentally swaps the sperm sample for his best friend's artificial insemination and later falls in love with her.

  • The Other Woman (2014)

    A romantic comedy about three women who team up to get revenge on the man who cheated on all of them.

  • Friends with Kids (2011)

    A comedy-drama about two best friends who decide to have a child together without being in a romantic relationship.

  • What to Expect When You're Expecting (2012)

    A romantic comedy-drama about five couples who are preparing for the birth of their children.

  • Baby Mama (2008)

    A comedy about a successful businesswoman who hires a surrogate mother to have her baby.

  • Knocked Up (2007)

    A comedy about a one-night stand that results in an unexpected pregnancy and the couple's attempts to navigate their relationship and impending parenthood.

  • The Back-Up Plan (2010)

    A romantic comedy about a woman who becomes pregnant via artificial insemination and later meets the man of her dreams.

  • Crazy, Stupid, Love (2011)

    A romantic comedy-drama about a man who seeks the help of a young woman to rekindle his relationship with his wife.

  • This Is 40 (2012)

    A comedy-drama about a couple who are turning 40 and struggling to balance their careers, family life, and relationship.

  • Father of the Bride (1991)

    A classic comedy about a man who is reluctant to let go of his daughter as she prepares to get married.

  • Playing for Keeps (2012)

    A romantic comedy-drama about a retired soccer player who moves to Virginia to reconnect with his ex-wife and son.

  • The Kids Are All Right (2010)

    A comedy-drama about a same-sex couple whose teenage children seek out their biological father and invite him into their lives.

  • Little Miss Sunshine (2006)

    A comedy-drama about a dysfunctional family who embarks on a road trip to get their daughter to a beauty pageant.

  • Instant Family (2018)

    A comedy-drama about a couple who decide to adopt three siblings and quickly realize that parenting is much harder than they expected.

  • Juno (2007)

    A comedy-drama about a teenage girl who becomes pregnant and decides to give the baby up for adoption to a couple who can't have children.

  • Away We Go (2009)

    A comedy-drama about a couple who are expecting their first child and travel around the country to find the perfect place to raise their family.

  • The Family Stone (2005)

    A romantic comedy-drama about a man who brings his uptight girlfriend home for the holidays and his family's unexpected reaction to her.

  • Parental Guidance (2012)

    A comedy about a couple who are asked to take care of their grandchildren and struggle to adapt to modern parenting methods.

  • Nine Months (1995)

    A romantic comedy-drama about a man who is shocked to learn that his girlfriend is pregnant and must quickly adjust to the idea of fatherhood.

  • Shotgun Wedding (2022)

    Darcy and Tom gather their families for the ultimate destination wedding but when the entire party is taken hostage, “’Til Death Do Us Part” takes on a whole new meaning in this hilarious, adrenaline-fueled adventure as Darcy and Tom must save their loved ones—if they don’t kill each other first.
